Frequently Asked Questions

1. What does SK Curtain Tapes specialize in?

SK Curtain Tapes specializes in manufacturing high-quality curtain tapes used in a variety of curtain designs. Our products are known for their durability, consistent quality, and suitability for both domestic and international markets.

2. Where is SK Curtain Tapes located?

We are headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. Our production facility is equipped with modern machinery to meet the growing demands of our clients worldwide.

3. Can I order samples before purchasing in bulk?

Yes, we offer sample tapes for evaluation. Please contact our sales team with your sample request, and we'll be happy to assist you.

4. How can I place an order?

You can place an order by contacting us directly through our website's contact form, emailing our sales department, or calling us. We will guide you through the ordering process step by step.

Yes, we export our curtain tapes to several countries. Our export team ensures smooth logistics and documentation for international shipments.

8. What is the minimum order quantity for any product?

The minimum order quantity (MOQ) depends on the specific product type. Generally, we maintain an MOQ that ensures cost-effective production and delivery. Please contact our team for product-specific MOQs.

9. What is the lead time for an order placed with SK Curtain Tapes?

Lead times vary based on order size and current production schedules. Typically, orders are fulfilled within 7–15 business days. We'll provide a clear timeline upon order confirmation.

Our standard payment terms are 50% advance and 50% before dispatch, unless otherwise agreed upon. We accept payments through bank transfers and other reliable channels.
